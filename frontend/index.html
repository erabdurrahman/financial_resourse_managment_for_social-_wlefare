About the Project

Solving the trust gap in social welfare

Traditional welfare systems are plagued by opacity — donors don't know where their money goes, and deserving beneficiaries are often overlooked due to manual, biased processes.

  • ✓ Full auditability — every transaction, application, and decision is logged and verifiable by all parties.
  • ✓ Algorithmic fairness — a priority-based system removes human bias from fund allocation decisions.
  • ✓ Community-driven — donors engage with real impact data, building sustained long-term giving habits.
  • ✓ Built for scale — whether managing ₹10 000 or ₹10 000 000, the system handles it with ease.
🎯

Problem we solve

Funds often go to well-connected individuals rather than the most needy. We replace connections with data-driven, transparent criteria.

💡

Our solution

A three-tier platform with role-based access, immutable audit logs, and a priority engine that objectively ranks aid applications.

🌱

Long-term impact

By building donor trust through transparency, we create a virtuous cycle that grows the welfare pool year after year.
